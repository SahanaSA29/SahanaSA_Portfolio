IRCTC Redesign
UI/UX Mobile App Case Study

IRCTC Rail Connect — App Redesign

Redesigned one of India's leading train-booking platforms by identifying usability issues such as cluttered screens, inconsistent navigation, weak visual hierarchy, and accessibility gaps. Applied Hick's Law, Fitts's Law, Gestalt Principles, and Miller's Law to define design goals. Delivered high-fidelity prototypes with Glassmorphism, gradient backgrounds, and intuitive feature grouping — resulting in improved clarity, usability, and user engagement.
